Finasteride

Medication is awaiting classification.

Finasteride does NOT lower testosterone levels, only DHT levels. It is associated with adverse mental health effects.

To save money, some people buy 5mg pills and split them into smaller parts. However, it is crucial to check the product monogram and make sure this is a good idea, as finasteride is cytotoxic and poses a risk of birth defects to pregnant women if they are exposed to finasteride powder via inhalation or through skin contact.
