Medications

Medication / Sourcing Information Main Use
Estradiol Injections Estrogen Cheapest way to start HRT with minimal to no side effects.
Estradiol Pills Estrogen The most common estrogen.
Estradiol Gel Estrogen The best alternative to injections or pills.
Estradiol Patches Estrogen Can have shortage problems.
Spironolactone Anti-Androgen The most common Anti-Androgen perscribed in north america, anecdotally hated.
Bicalutamide Anti-Androgen Has the least major side effects of the anti-androgens.
Cyproterone Acetate Anti-Androgen Will tank testosterone levels, with some safety information to keep in mind.
GnRH Agonists Anti-Androgen One of the best anti-androgens, however difficult to obtain and can be expensive. Can also be used as puberty blockers.
Progesterone Capsules Progesterone Easy to obtain however when taken orally (you can take them using... other methods) it can cause you to be sleepy.
Progesterone Gel Progesterone A lot harder to obtain than other methods but a good alternative to rectal or injections.
Progesterone Injections Progesterone Great way to get progesterone however it requires frequent injection (daily or every other day).
Finasteride Hair Loss Decreases DHT levels to prevent hair loss (NOT an anti-androgen) and is associated with mental health effects.
Dutasteride Hair Loss Decreases DHT levels to prevent hair loss (NOT an anti-androgen) and is associated with mental health effects.
Raloxifene SERM Complicated read info page before use.
Domperidone Dompamine Antagonist Do not use for breast growth, useful to induce lactation.
